Current Positions

I currently hold two part-time research assistant positions, one of which is partly supported by the Blue Waters Student Internship Program (BWSIP).

  1. I work at the Simulation-Based Engineering Lab (SBEL) under Professor Dan Negrut and Dr. Arman Pazouki. At SBEL I assist in the investigation of parallel computing techniques for GPU, shared and distributed memory architectures and their application to fluid-solid interaction problems. I regularly report my work at SBEL to the BWSIP. As part of the program I will be submitting a paper about my work and experience throughout BWSIP to the Journal of Computational Science Education.

  2. I also work in Professor Vikas Singh Group, where I collaborate with Prof. Singh and Vamsi Ithapu on topics related to statistical analysis of brain imaging datasets. My undergraduate thesis will be based on all the work I did in Prof. Singh's group.
